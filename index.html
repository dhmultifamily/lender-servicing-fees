The servicing fee is 10 basis points (0.10%) of the unpaid principal balance, paid monthly over the loan term. Matthews' share of this fee depends on loan size via the Essex Financial Services partnership: loans up to $5M → Matthews gets 10%; loans over $5M → Matthews gets 25%. Matthews' share is then split 50/50 between the company and the originating agent.
